About Us

Who We Are

We are a club for Brunonians interested in chess. We play chess, analyze games, compete in tournaments, and do other fun stuff! Whether you're a complete novice or a seasoned pro, we'd love to help you on your journey to chess mastery.

Meeting Information

We currently meet weekly on Mondays from 8 PM to 10 PM. The location can vary from week to week, so either ask a member the day of the meeting or join our mailing list to know where the meeting is being held every time.
